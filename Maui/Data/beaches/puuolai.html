Puu Olai

"Little Beach", as Puu Olai is best known, is famous for being "clothing optional".

Itʻs often hard to get pictures of popular beaches without people on them, and doubly so for this one, as I didnʻt want any (even marginal) adult content on the site.

You get to Puu Olai by going to the north end of Big Beach and climbing over a small rise. This beach is quite small, and very well worn. On busy days it can be covered with people doing all manner of things.

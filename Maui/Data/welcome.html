Exploring Maui

Maui has a lot to discover; this is a personal recording of both things done and things yet-to-do.

When you click on a location you get a picture (or a few) representing that spot.

If there is a short story to go along, you will see the "Story" button and can, optionally, read a bit.

Exit this screen, to the map, using the X in the upper right.

Happy exploring.

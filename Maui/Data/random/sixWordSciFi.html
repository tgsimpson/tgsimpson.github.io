Six Word SciFi

Inspired by Wired Magazines Sep 2022 issues, where they challenged readers to "In six words, write a story about a galaxy far, far away."

Their best entries were (first one was the winning one):

You turned left at Sirius B?!
42 was definitely not the answer.
Dear humans, nobody wants unsolicited nudes.
Humans! There goes the dang neighborhood.
Is this DC's or Marvel's Universe?
"The robots are BLEEDING!" she screamed.
Directions to transdimensional left luggage office?
